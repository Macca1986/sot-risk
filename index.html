Services

Executive & Close Protection

Proactive security solutions for individuals, families, and executives, ensuring safety through risk management, threat assessment, and skilled protection teams.

Risk Assessment

Comprehensive evaluations of individual and location-based threats, infrastructure accessibility, and real-time operational risks for both individuals and corporations.

Counter Terrorism Advisory

Identification of potential terrorist threats to venues and locations, with tailored countermeasures to mitigate vulnerabilities.

Security Management Consultancy

Expert advisory services on security protocols, vulnerability assessments, training, and technology recommendations for enhanced protection.

Media Escort Services

Protection and advisory support for media teams operating in semi-permissive environments, ensuring secure and effective coverage.

VIP Escape & Evacuation

Discreet evacuation support for clients in semi-permissive environments who prefer not to have a visible security presence.

High Value Property Escort

Secure transportation and protection for valuable assets such as art, jewelry, vehicles, and rare goods.

Humanitarian Logistics Support

Safe and efficient transport of essential humanitarian aid, including food, shelter, and medical supplies, to high-risk locations.

Hostile Environment Awareness Training (HEAT)

Specialized training for high-risk travel, covering situational awareness, survival strategies, and conflict de-escalation.

Cultural Sensitivity Training

Training to enhance understanding of foreign cultures and security risks, ensuring safe and effective international engagement.

Concierge & Escort Services

Personalized non-security concierge assistance for individuals navigating foreign locations.

Surveillance & Counter-Surveillance Services

Detection and prevention of corporate espionage through expert surveillance services.

QRF Emergency Support

Rapid emergency response support for existing security teams to facilitate safe client evacuation in high-risk situations.
